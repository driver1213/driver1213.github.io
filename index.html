About Me

Hi, I'm Diego Rivera.

I'm a veteran turned web developer. Changing careers has been one of the best decisions in my life but also the most intimidating. In September 2019, I was honorably discharged of active duty and was about to start a career in civilian law enforcement. Coding in general seemed so difficult to me. When I found out the military was willing to pay for my training, I jumped on the opportunity without hesitation. The military gave me great skills, for example attention to detail and discipline to finish the job, which has helped me a lot in web development. As my skills grow so does the passion to get better. When I'm not working I love hiking, playing soccer and pretty much any outside activity I can do with my family.
